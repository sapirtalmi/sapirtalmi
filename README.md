## 🎧 Mellow — Audio Reasoning Under Real-World Constraints
A real deep learning research project, end-to-end. Training a 167M-parameter audio-language model from scratch under real hardware constraints, then diving into loss curves, t-SNE visualizations, and spectrograms to understand what the model actually learned. Seeing DSP fundamentals and transformer behavior come alive in real audio data, from raw waveforms to model outputs to insights.

[🔗 GitHub](https://github.com/sapirtalmi/mellow_apdl_project)
